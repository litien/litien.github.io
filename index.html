Design Process


I do the End-to-End Design process depending on project needs. Here are the areas that I'm passionate about:




Ideation

Generate concepts, discover users' needs based on research results, and align goals within the business requirements and stakeholders' expectations.

Architecture

Deal with page structures, user flows and case scenarios before touching the design, and handle how users transit smoothly from one state to the next.

Visual

Craft graphic elements and play around with typography and color to ensure a consistent design language is applied across the product.

Front-end Development

Build what I design and balance the latest technology with intuitive design into usable, useful and user-friendly applications.
