Seattle Flowers

Website Redesign

Seattle Flowers provides elegant flower bouquets and delivery service throughout Seattle. I like flowers, because they bring happiness and add a lot of color to the place. This is a self-initiated project for practicing UI and minimalistic design.


The previous website design was too heavy and not intuitive. The new look is focused on the content, and allows people to quickly order bouquets without extra clicks. The layout and navigation are keep in the same structure from previous version to make transition to new design smoother.
