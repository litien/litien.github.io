YogaPanda

Startup Weekend 2nd Place

YogaPanda is an mobile app that helps yogis find next available classes at the best yoga studios in the Greater Seattle area, book classes directly from phone, pay as you go and save money along the way. During the EuroSeattle Startup Weekend, I created the mobile design solution with another designer and collaborated with business owners and developers, and the team won the 2nd place. (Live website: http://yogapanda.co/ )


Download on the App Store



ROLE

Mobile UI/UX Design

METHODS

Market Research, Survey, Interview, Sketches, UI Flow, Mockups

DURATION

Jan 2015 (2 Days)



Find Affordable Yoga Classes near you

The Yoga Panda marketplace makes it easy for you to find yoga classes near you at various yoga studios all around the Greater Seattle area. Prices vary depending on the popularity of each class.

Find the perfect yoga class for you

The Yoga Panda app makes it easy for you to narrow down the list of yoga classes based on the type of yoga you are interested in. You can also filter yoga classes by price, distance, yoga teacher, yoga studio or time of the day.

Get Access to the best yoga studios in town

The Yoga Panda app provides you access to the best yoga studios in town. Try different yoga studios, different styles of yoga, different yoga teachers. Regardless of the time of the day or the neighborhood you are in, Yoga Panda has your back.

Pay as you go directly from your phone

The Yoga Panda App makes it easy for you to sign up for yoga classes and pay for them directly from your phone. No membership, no contract and no waste. You only pay for the yoga classes you take.

Easy Check in for your yoga classes

Forget about long and lengthy sign up and check in processes when you get to the yoga studio. Going to your yoga class is as simple as scanning your phone at the front desk. Yoga Panda takes care of the rest. It is that simple.

    • Why use YogaPanda?

  • The Right Yoga Class For You: Compare local options, teachers, prices, time, and styles of yoga.
  • Pay As You Go: Don't pay every month. Total freedom from subscriptions, no monthly payments.
  • Book From The App: Sign up instantly for the next available class.
