YogaPanda

Startup Weekend 2nd Place

YogaPanda is an mobile app that helps yogis find next available classes at the best yoga studios in the Greater Seattle area, book classes directly from phone, pay as you go and save money along the way. During the EuroSeattle Startup Weekend, I created the mobile design solution with another designer and collaborated with business owners and developers, and the team won the 2nd place.


Note: On October 2016, the service is shut down. Read the startup journey written by the founder Othmane Rahmouni.

ROLE

Mobile UI/UX Design

METHODS

Market Research, Survey, Interview, Sketches, UI Flow, Mockups

DURATION

Jan 2015 (2 Days)

Problem

Yoga studios:

  • Struggling financially
  • Looking to attract customers

    • Users' voice:

  • “I want to do yoga, but I am not sure what class to go to.”
  • “I don’t have time, I am not sure if there is a class exactly.”
  • “It is too expensive, I only go if I can get a Groupon.”

    • Why use YogaPanda?

  • The Right Yoga Class For You: Compare local options, teachers, prices, time, and styles of yoga.
  • Pay As You Go: Don't pay every month. Total freedom from subscriptions, no monthly payments.
  • Book From The App: Sign up instantly for the next available class.
